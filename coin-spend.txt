Age:
- bronze adds/subtracts 1 yr
- silver change to any age between 7-16
- gold to change to any age
Body:
- bronze add/subtract 2 from your roll
- silver picks your body type
Physical & Magical Stats:
- bronze adds 1 to a stat
- silver adds 2 to a stat
- gold adds 4 to a stat
Specialisation:
- bronze add/subtract 1 from your roll
- silver swaps to any specialisation 1 before/after your roll
-- if 19-20, can only swap to 19-20
- gold lets you pick any specialisation
Weapon:
- bronze add/subtract 4 from roll
- silver picks your weapon
- gold gives a second weapon
Outfit:
- bronze add/subtract 4 from roll
- silver picks your outfit
Power:
- bronze add/subtract 2 from your roll
- silver lets you pick any power
- gold lets you choose a second power
Perks:
- bronze shifts a roll to the opposite category (support to combat)
- silver lets you pick freely from the same category
- gold lets you select two bonus perks from either category
