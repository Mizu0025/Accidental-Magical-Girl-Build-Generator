## üí∞ Starting Information (File 2)
You start with **3 Silver Coins** and **4 Bronze Coins** (implied from the text: "three silver coins and four bronze coins" after coming to terms with it).
It is best to spend coins now, as they won't have as much effect later.

## üìù Initial Rolls (Roll 11d20)

### **Roll 1: AGE (File 2)**
* You are now **immortal** and will no longer age. If you die, you become a spirit and can eventually recreate your body.
* **Formula:** Age is **6 + [Roll]**.
* **Roll Range:** Treat 1-10 as is. Subtract 10 from rolls of 11-20.
* **Coins:**
    * **Bronze:** +/- 1 year.
    * **Silver:** Choose any age between 7 and 16.
    * **Gold:** Choose any age you wish.

### **Roll 2: BODY (File 2)**
* Minutia (ethnicity, hair/eye color, etc.) can be chosen. Overall shape is up to chance.
* **Coins:**
    * **Bronze:** +/- 2 from your roll.
    * **Silver:** Pick your body type.

| Roll Range | Body Type | Description | Bonus |
| :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **1-6** | Underdeveloped | Smaller, sickly, thin, or much younger looking. | +1 LCK or MAG |
| **7-14** | Average | Average for your age. No standout features. | +1 AGI or VIT |
| **15-20** | Overdeveloped | Taller, bigger, wider, more muscular, appears older or has precocious puberty. | +1 STR or VIT |

## üåü Stats

### **Physical Stats (Start at 4) (File 3)**
* **Bronze:** +1 to a stat.
* **Silver:** +2 to a stat.
* **Gold:** +4 to a stat.
* **Human Average is 2** (4 is average adult).

| Stat | Focus | Benchmarks (Select Examples) |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **STR** | Force/Exertion (Health & Speed) | 10: Refrigerator, 18: Average sedan |
| **AGI** | Dexterity/Coordination (Defense & Speed) | 10: Easily hit a bullseye at 20 feet, 18: Easily hit a double bull at 20 feet with a thrown fly |

### **Mystical Stats (Start at 4) (File 3)**
* **Bronze:** +1 to a stat.
* **Silver:** +2 to a stat.
* **Gold:** +4 to a stat.
* **Human Average is 2**.

| Stat | Focus | Benchmarks (Fire Example) |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **MAG** | Magical Power/Mana (Mana capacity/Skill) | 10: Shoot blazing columns of fire, 20: Self propulsion, beams of heat, and a fire aura. |
| **LCK** | Favored by Fate (Things just go right) | No hard benchmark. |

## ‚öîÔ∏è Roll 4: WEAPON (File 9)
* **Indestructible** and can always be called to your side.
* Ranged and Mystic weapons create their own ammunition/mana. Ranged weapons can use normal ammunition for their type.
* You gain at least novice-level understanding of the weapon if you don't already have it.
* **Bronze:** +/- 4 from your roll.
* **Silver:** Pick your weapon.
* **Gold:** Select a **SECOND** weapon.

| Roll Range | Weapon Type | Examples | Stat Bonus |
